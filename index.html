Trending Courses

Chakra Music Therapy

Chakra music therapy helps you to balance your chakras. This therapy uses musical notes to improve your mental and physical well-being. Chakra music therapy will teach you to live in full harmony with yourself.

Light Music

Light music is the form of Indian classical music where words are arranged in a decorative rhythmic style. Bhajans, folk songs, Ghazal and many more musical forms come under light music. Light music elevates your stressful mood to a delightful mood.

Harmonium Classes

Harmonium lessons are the source of in-depth knowledge of notes and scales, one can grab the benefit of playing an instrument and understanding any form of music. Harmonium playing will cultivate creativity and produce perseverance in yourself
