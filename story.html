Dhanashree Kulkarni


Is a talented vocalist who nurtures a deep passion for Indian Classical Music. An ardent singer who believes that learning is a continual process, she has dedicated a greater part of her life to music education and propagation.

Dhanashree obtained an M. A. in Indian Classical Music, followed by a Diploma in Light Music. She has learned under the inspired tutelage of Gurus who have acquired global recognition. Her training took her to the doorstep of musical maestros including Mrs Shobha Abhyankar (Indian Classical Music), Pt. Chandrashekhar Deshpande (Natyasangeet) and Pt. Yeshwant Deo & Shrinivas Khale (Light Music). She has also received special music mentoring from Pt. Chhota Gandharva.

Captivating Performances


Dhanashree is a gifted singer who is blessed with a smooth, silky voice. She captivates the audience with her cheerful tones; from a classical raga to a feet-tapping melody and a devotional bhajan, she delivers each piece with amazing versatility and flair. Dhanashree keeps a busy schedule; her calendar is bookmarked with invites to perform at corporate events, cultural programs and grand family

Awards

Dhanashree's singing talent has earned her recognition, both from the music fraternity as well as the community. She has been honoured with numerous awards, some at the hands of stalwarts like Manik Varma, Shashi Kapoor and Babasaheb Purandare. Some of the noted accolades include:

 Ravivar Sakal Vishesh Gaurav' Award
  Deccan Youth Club Award
  Karale trust (Sangli) Award
  Sangeet Seva Award is given by the Akhil Bharatiya Deshastha Rigvediya Brahman Sanstha
  Excellent Vocalist Award at the 21 International Drama Competition, organized by the Delhi Maharashtrian Educational & Cultural Club

Key highlights

 Independent vocal performances on Akashwani & Doordarshan
 Classical rendition at the 17 Youth festival held at Ratlam(Madhya Pradesh), organized by the South Central Cultural Zone, Nagpur
 Classical singing program held at the Gateway of India, Mumbai organized by the Directorate of Cultural Affairs, Govt. Of Maharashtra on the occasion of Independence Day and Maharashtra
Day
 In 2018 she was honoured to perform at Sawai Gandharv Music Festival, Kundagol
 Recently in the 2019 Holi Festival she was blessed to perform at Swami Haridas Samadhi temple, Vridavan.

Other Milestones

Dhanashree is not only a versatile singer; she is also a brilliant music composer. She has composed the music for Sanjay Upadhye's music CD titled “ Inde Mauchi Gani”. Dhanashree has also created the music for the program 'Journey - Amusical family', which features tracks by singers Anand Bhate, Savani Shende and Prajakta Ranade.

Anahata Music Atelier is an institution based in Pune, devoted to education and training in Indian Classical Music.
