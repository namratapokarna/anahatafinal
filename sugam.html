Light Music

In Anahat Music Atelier, various types of intelligible music such as Bhavgeet, Bhakti Geet, Film Songs (Hindi, Marathi), Ghazal and Folk Songs are taught in the relevant style and there is also a facility to fully prepare and sit for all the exams for Sugam Sangeet.

In this, the singer has to do justice to the composition composed by the composer and lyricist through his singing.

Class benefits:

  • Preparation of sentimentality in a song.
  • Preparation of Bhakti (Devotional Feel).
  • Preparation of meaning within the lyrics.
  • Preparation of "बाझ” (Vibe) in each singing genre.

 Sing along with instruments.
  Specific light music singing techniques.    
  Learn proper method of singing.

Who is this course for?

  • Want to learn the types of Geet(गीत) in Sugam Sangeet.

  • The one who wants to appear or prepare for the exam up to Sugam Sangeet Visharad.

  • Candidates who want to get trained as a performer.

What will you learn?

  • Learn all the types of songs in Sugam Sangeet with features and with the specific singing style.

  • Exams and accompaniment of Sugam sangeet will be taken care of simultaneously.

  • Will be able to sing any song type with confidence.

  • Opportunity to sing along with all instruments.

Personal Session

  • Your favorite songs will be arranged in individual sessions.

  • 12 sessions per month

  • Certificate will be provided after completion of course

Group Session

  • Even in the group session personal attention will be provided while teaching.

  • 12 sessions per month

  • Certificate will be provided after completion of course


Do you offer trial lessons?

We do offer trial sessions. Register yourself for a trial session. Is it possible for anyone to learn the music irrespective of age/voice quality? We definitely believe that one can learn music as long as there is a passion for learning, as well as practice on a daily basis.

Can a student enroll in multiple courses?
The Academy allows its students to enroll in multiple courses. Note that classes are subject to the availability of time slots and teachers.
I don't have any musical background or ability; can I still help my child practice?
Yes. Even if you don't have a musical background, you can ask the teacher for advice on how to help your child practice. By simply monitoring and practicings each day the student can achieve progress. Parents can accompany children for a better understanding of music lessons and musical gestures.
Are solo performance opportunities encouraged?

Yes. Opportunities for solo performance include weekly studio classes which provide a format for students to present single works. The weekly music seminar showcases superior student performers and is attended by the entire Anahata Music Atelier’s student body. Where everyone presents partial and entire concert programs, respectively. Ambitious students will be given an opportunity for a special performance.
