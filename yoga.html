Yoga And Pranayama Classes

Yoga brings together the human body's three most vital aspects: the physical body, mental peace, and, last but not least, the soul. These three elements keep the human body in balance, allowing us to go about our daily lives without interruption. Yoga isn't the only thing that can help us relax; music can also help us relax. We teach yoga and music at Anahata Music Atelier. You can achieve balance and concentration here. This course can also assist you in achieving a specific mental state.

Yoga is a systematic practice of physical exercise, breathing control, relaxation, diet control, positive thinking and meditation aimed at creating harmony in the body, mind and environment.

Benefits of Yoga and Pranayama classes:

  • Clear Vocal Tones And Voice.
  • Breath control.
  • In Head And Neck Movements While Singing.
